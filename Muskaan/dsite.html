Muskaan

"SMILE and GRATITUDE makes you happier''. The Rotaract Club of RVCE brings to you MUSKAAN, a heartfelt event where you are the reason behind someone's smile! You can join the chain by following these simple steps:1. Give a small token of appreciation to your driver, maid, sweeper, anyone who works to make your life easier. It could be anything, a box of sweets, a t-shirt, a pair of shoes, utensils, books, etc. 2. Click a picture with them and upload it on your instagram account as a story and tag @rotaractrvce with the following hashtags #thesmilegiveaway #DilKiMuskaan3. Nominate 3 other people whom you want to join the smile giveaway chain and ask them to do the same.4. Follow the official page of Rotaract Club of RVCE @rotaractrvce where the pictures will be uploaded on 20th of March 2020, International Day of Happiness.
